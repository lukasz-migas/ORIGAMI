UniDec

UniDec is a Bayesian deconvolution algorithm that can be used to deconvolute mass spectra and ion mobility-mass spectra. It was developed by Dr. Michael Marty and is freely available under the modified BSD licence. I would highly recommend you check-out full version of UniDec and MetaUniDec software. You can find it https://github.com/michaelmarty/UniDec/releases

Citation

If you use UniDec in your work, please cite their work: M. T. Marty, A. J. Baldwin, E. G. Marklund, G. K. A. Hochberg, J. L. P. Benesch, C. V. Robinson, Anal. Chem. 2015, 87, 4370-4376.
